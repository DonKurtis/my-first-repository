Elephant

The Elephant is one of the world's largest land mammals on the planet. Known for their large trunks, sharp tusks and long memories. They are mainly found across Africa, more specifcally Eastern Africa. They are also known to be found across South-East Asia

Tiger

The Tigers are the largest wild cats in the world. Famous for their orange and black stripes and distinguished markings across their face, these majestic beings are found all across Asia and even as far East as Siberia, home to the largest Tiger of all.
